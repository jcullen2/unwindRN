# App Store Listing

## Category & framing

- Category: Medical or Productivity — NOT Health & Fitness, NOT Lifestyle. Category choice is a positioning act; sitting next to Calm frames us as Calm.
- The listing sells the wedge (career record + knowledge), never the platform buffet. One spine: record → patterns → pride.

## Title & subtitle (30 chars each)

- Title: `unwindRN — Nurse Shift Logbook` (or `unwindRN: The Nurse's Logbook`). "Logbook" is the highest-value word we can own.
- Subtitle candidates: `Every shift, on the record` / `Your career, counted` / `Shift log, debrief, milestones`.
- Ban in title/subtitle: wellness, mental health, calm, relax, therapy, self-care, mindfulness.

## Keyword field (not user-visible — broader net allowed)

nurse, RN, shift, logbook, shift tracker, nursing career, night shift, debrief, ICU, ER, travel nurse, milestones. Wellness-adjacent keywords are acceptable HERE for discovery (nurse burnout, decompress) because the field is invisible — but the visible listing never echoes them.

## Description structure

1. **First two lines (visible before "more"):** the one-sentence position, adapted. Example: "unwindRN is how nurses know their own careers. Every shift counted, every pattern surfaced — written in the minutes after clock-out." Never open with feelings, speed, or a question.
2. **The logbook paragraph:** backfill, career stats, milestones, the ~.
3. **The debrief paragraph:** aviation register, elasticity ("as short as your good nights, as long as your bad ones"), voice companion for depth.
4. **What it gives back:** 3 career-facing insight examples as bullets.
5. **The inversion paragraph:** no manager dashboard, no HR report; on-device voice; scrubber; export/delete.
6. **Plain footer:** free during beta; not medical care or therapy; 988 note. (The denial lives at the bottom, once, factually.)

## Screenshots (order = argument)

1. Logbook heatfield with career totals
2. Home: career shifts held, milestone countdown
3. A shift card (shareable proof)
4. An insight line over real-looking data
5. Clock-out taps
6. Debrief screen (last, not first)

Caption each with a counted claim, not a feeling: "1,153 shifts, on the record" not "Feel lighter after every shift."

## What's-new / release notes

Same voice, small scale: "The logbook now counts charge shifts separately. Milestone cards are shareable. Nothing about you leaves your phone — still." Plain, counted, no exclamation inflation.
