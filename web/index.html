How it works

Twenty seconds. Then sleep.

No journaling homework. No wellness lecture. A ritual short enough to survive a brutal night — and worth more every time you do it.

01

Clock out by taps

Hours, load, and what tonight had — code, a loss, a good save. A few taps and it's kept. “Save without another word” is always one tap, and never judged.

02

Talk it down — when you want to

A debrief partner fluent in your specialty listens like a colleague at the end of the hall: one question at a time, no clinical labels, no chirpy nonsense. Name the win, the weight, the lesson — then go to bed.

03

The record writes itself

Every night becomes a clean entry — date, hours, load, tags, and the one line worth keeping. Your months fill a heatfield. Your career fills a logbook.

The logbook

The career nobody else counts.

You know your years. unwindRN counts the rest. Tell it your history and it starts the record where your career actually started — a nurse eight years in has held roughly 1,150 shifts — then it grows, night by night: hours held, nights survived, milestones as they pass.

It's the professional memory no hospital ever kept for you. And it's yours — export it, or delete every trace, any time.

Built to be trusted

Safe for you. Safe for your license.

Your voice never leaves the phone

Speech is transcribed on-device by iOS. Audio is never uploaded and never stored — and the words you speak in a debrief aren't kept either. Only the record you approve is saved.

Patients stay unnamed

unwindRN is engineered to keep patient information out of your record — by design, by prompt, and by an automated scrubber. Talk about your night, not their identity.

Not therapy — and honest about it

No diagnoses, no clinical labels, no pretending to be treatment. If a night is heavier than an app should hold, 988 is surfaced immediately — never gated, never delayed.

No streaks. No guilt.

No red badges, no “you missed a day.” Skip a week and the record just waits. The only pressure in this app is the kind you put down.

Questions nurses ask

The honest answers.

Is unwindRN therapy?

No — and it never pretends to be. It's a debrief partner and a logbook: a place to put the shift down and keep your record. It doesn't diagnose, treat, or replace real support. If you're in crisis, call or text 988 — the app keeps that one tap away, always.

What happens to what I say?

Your voice is transcribed on your phone — audio never leaves it. The transcript text is used to generate your partner's reply and the draft record, then it's gone; we don't store conversations. The only thing saved is the shift entry you approve. Full details in the privacy policy.

Could this cause trouble with HIPAA or my license?

unwindRN is built for the opposite. It never asks for patient information, the partner deflects it if offered, and an automated scrubber strips identifiers (rooms, beds, record numbers) before anything can be saved. Your record describes your night — never a patient's identity.

What does it cost?

Free during the beta. If that ever changes, you'll hear it from us plainly and first — no surprise paywalls on your own record.

Android?

iPhone first, so the beta can be excellent instead of everywhere. Android is on the roadmap — join the waitlist and tell us that's you, and you'll be first to know.
